About

Welcome to the Countrynomics. The Countrynomics aggregates important economic indicators and provides analytics for G20 countries. Our service is intended for people who are interested in analyzing macroeconomics trend but do not have access to paid service providers. We use freely available data from international organizations such as IMF, OECD, and World Trade Organization.

The Countrynomics was launched in September 2019 and it is fairly new. We will be providing more features and data in the near future. Please let us know if you have any suggestions.
